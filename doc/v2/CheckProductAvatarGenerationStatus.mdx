---
title: "Check Product Avatar Generation Status"
openapi: "GET /v2/product_avatar/generation/{batch_id}"
---

Get the status and results of product avatar image generation by batch ID.

## Example Usage

```bash
curl --request GET \
  --url 'https://api.jogg.ai/v2/product_avatar/generation/batch_123456' \
  --header 'x-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY'
```
